ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Family business in Indonesia have seen much stronger growth than the global average over the last year and are vey bullish about future growth. 83% have grown in the last 12 months (vs.65% globally) and 96% are aiming to grow over the next five years (vs. 85% globally). (PWC Family Business Survey, 2014)

Understanding entrepreneurship and innovation in the context of family business is highly relevant from a practical point of view because there is a growing body of evidence showing that family firms use entrepreneurship and innovation to nurture their competitive advantage and to overcome economic and financial downturns. (IFB Research Foundation, 2016
